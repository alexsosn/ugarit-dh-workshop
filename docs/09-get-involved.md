## Contribute to the corpus

Ugaritic studies is small enough that **one careful person makes a visible
difference** — and large enough that there is real work to do. Ways in, from
lightest to deepest:

- **Spot and report errors.** Reading a transliteration and something looks off?
  Open an issue on the relevant repo. Corrections are contributions.
- **Help annotate.** CUC currently has no lemma / part-of-speech layer; building and
  checking that is exactly the kind of task where a philologist + AI pair works
  well (see notebook `3c` and `docs/08-modern-toolkit.md`).
- **Build a small tool.** You now have the recipe from
  `docs/hour3-agent-runbook.md`. Convert a source you care about, share the method
  (not licensed data).
- **Extend the resource map.** Found a corpus, image archive, or translation we
  missed? Add it to `docs/00-resources.md`.
